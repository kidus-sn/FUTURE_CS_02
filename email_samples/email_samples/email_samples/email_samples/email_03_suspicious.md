# Email 03 - SUSPICIOUS

## Email Content

From: rewards@amazon-offers.net

Subject: Congratulations! You Have Been Selected

Hello Customer,

You have been selected to receive a special reward from Amazon.

Please review the attached offer and confirm your eligibility.

Offer expires soon.

Best regards,

Rewards Team

## Analysis

### Sender Check

The sender does not use an official Amazon domain.

### Urgency Check
The email encourages quick action by mentioning that the offer expires soon.

### Link and Attachment Check
The email references an offer without clearly explaining its source.

### Request for Sensitive Information
No direct request for passwords is made.

### Grammar and Spelling
The email is generally well written.

## Classification

SUSPICIOUS

## Reason

The sender domain appears unofficial and the message uses promotional language and urgency. While there is no direct phishing attempt visible, the email should be treated with caution.
