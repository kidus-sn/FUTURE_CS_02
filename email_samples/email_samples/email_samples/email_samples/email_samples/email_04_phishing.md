# Email 04 - PHISHING

## Email Content

From: security-update@paypal-verification.net

Subject: Urgent Account Verification Required

Dear Customer,

Your PayPal account has been temporarily restricted due to unusual activity. To restore access, verify your account immediately using the link below:

http://paypal-account-verify.net

Failure to verify within 24 hours may result in account suspension.

Thank you,

## Analysis

### Sender Check
The sender does not use an official PayPal domain.

### Urgency Check
The email creates pressure by threatening account suspension.

### Link Check
The link does not belong to the official PayPal website.

### Request for Sensitive Information
The email attempts to direct users to a site where credentials may be collected.

### Grammar and Spelling
The message appears professional, which is common in modern phishing attacks.

## Classification

PHISHING

## Reason

The sender domain is fraudulent, the email uses urgency, and the embedded link directs users to a suspicious website that may be used to steal credentials.
