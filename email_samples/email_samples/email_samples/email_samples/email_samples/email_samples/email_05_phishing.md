# Email 05 - PHISHING

## Email Content

From: microsoft-support@security-alerts365.com

Subject: Password Expiration Notice

Dear User,

Your Microsoft account password will expire today. To prevent account deactivation, click the link below and update your password immediately:

http://microsoft-password-reset-security.com

Failure to update your password may result in loss of access to your account.

Microsoft Support Team

## Analysis

### Sender Check
The sender does not use an official Microsoft domain.

### Urgency Check
The email creates panic by claiming immediate account deactivation.

### Link Check
The link directs users to a non-Microsoft website.

### Request for Sensitive Information
The email attempts to collect account credentials.

### Grammar and Spelling
The message is professionally written but contains classic phishing characteristics.

## Classification

PHISHING

## Reason

The sender address is suspicious, the email uses urgency and fear tactics, and the link directs users to an unofficial website designed to collect credentials.
